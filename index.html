  • About

    The Maadi International Choir features singers from over 12 nationalities, drawing from the Maadi community as well as Greater Cairo. Led by director Barbara Comar, the choir puts on two annual performances open to the public, including Winter and Spring. The choir is also available for festivals and private events.
